Bestie was created with one mission: to help young adults navigate the complex world of relationships with confidence and authenticity.

Our Story

Bestie started as a personal project when our founder struggled with communication in their own relationships. After reading countless self-help books and attending therapy, they realized there was a gap in accessible relationship guidance.

We combined cutting-edge AI technology with evidence-based relationship psychology to create an app that's both fun to use and genuinely helpful. Today, Bestie has helped over 50,000 users build stronger, healthier relationships.
